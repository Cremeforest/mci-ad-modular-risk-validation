# Claims and limitations

## Supported claims

- Completed internal development and NACC external validation.
- The frozen ADNI-trained model retained moderate external discrimination in NACC under a no-ADAS13 setting.
- Raw frozen probabilities were underestimated under external cohort shift.
- Cross-fitted local Platt recalibration improved calibration without retraining the prediction model.
- Suitable for retrospective risk stratification analysis and methodological demonstration.

## Unsupported claims

- Full-module external validation in NACC.
- Clinical deployment readiness.
- Externally well-calibrated raw frozen probabilities.
- NACC-retrained model performance.
- Rich longitudinal external validation.
- Causal clinical feature importance.
- Universal superiority over all baselines.
